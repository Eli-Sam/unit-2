Tourists of the Caribbean Map

This is a map showing the "International tourism, number of arrivals" by year, and I decided to map the numbers for the Caribbean. The data comes from The World Bank Group(See the readme in the data folder). The data is from 2000 to 2020. Explore the tourist arrivals to the Caribbean!

Instructions: To use the map you may step through with the slider looking year by year. You can use either the stepper buttons or drag the slider. Clicking on the countires will allow you to view the arrivals by that country.
